We are a group of non-partisan and non-profit university researchers.
This HIT is part of a larger study that we are leading on MTurk over 2021-2023.


Instructions

The HIT has a base reward of $4.00 and a bonus of maximum $1.50. Your total payment will depend on decisions you will take during the HIT.
The expected completion time is 15 minutes.


The task is divided into four parts:

  • Part 1: A short survey about yourself.
  • Part 2: A counting task in which you can earn a bonus.
  • Part 3: Answering a few opinion questions.
  • Part 4: An item recognition task in which you can earn a bonus.


Please be aware that:

  • All questions are mandatory.
  • You need to complete the entire study to receive your reward.
  • Reponses that appear random or negligent will likely result in HIT rejection.
